# Flipkart Sales Performance Dashboard (Power BI)

## Project Overview
This project analyzes Flipkart sales data to provide insights into performance across products, regions, and payment methods. The dashboard is interactive and visually presents key metrics using **Power BI**.

## Problem Statement
Flipkart aims to:
- Track sales performance over time across product categories and regions.
- Understand seasonal trends and effects of sales campaigns or festivals.
- Analyze customer satisfaction and ratings to identify high-performing and underperforming products.
- Gain insights into payment method preferences and their impact on sales.
- Assess regional sales distribution for growth opportunities.

## Files in Repo
- `dashboard.pbix` → Power BI interactive dashboard  
- `Flipkart_Sales_Data.xlsx` → Dataset containing sales records  
- `Problem_Statement.docx` → Flipkart sales performance problem statement  

## Tools & Technologies Used
- **Power BI**: For interactive dashboard and visualizations  
- **Excel**: Data cleaning, preparation, and initial analysis  

## Dashboard Features
- **Total Sales, Average Sales, Average Rating, Quantity Sold** metrics at a glance.  
- **Top Product Categories**: Bar chart showing highest-selling categories.  
- **Sales Trend Over Time**: Line chart to visualize daily/monthly sales trends.  
- **Payment Method Analysis**: Donut chart showing usage percentages of COD, UPI, Credit Card, Debit Card, Net Banking.  
- **Sales by Region**: Map visualization showing sales distribution across regions.  
- **Quantity Sold by Product Category**: Horizontal bar chart.  
- **Sales Performance by Rating**: Insights into how customer ratings correlate with sales.  
- **Sales Breakdown by Region and Payment Method**: Combined chart for region-wise payment analysis.  

## Insights (Example)
- Certain product categories like Electronics and Grocery show consistently high sales.  
- Payment method preference impacts total sales; e.g., Debit Card and UPI are most popular.  
- Sales peak during festival periods, indicating seasonal trends.  
- Regions like North and South India contribute significantly to total sales.  
- Customer ratings provide insights for product improvements and marketing strategies.  

## Usage Steps
1. Open `Flipkart_Sales_Data.xlsx` for raw sales data.  
2. Load dataset into Power BI Desktop (`dashboard.pbix`).  
3. Explore dashboard filters like Product Category, Region, Payment Method.  
4. Interact with charts to gain actionable insights.
